# Nizhnemaryino

Nizhnemaryino

**Nizhnemaryino** (Russian: Нижнемарьино) is a rural locality (a selo) in Tresorukovskoye Rural Settlement, Liskinsky District, Voronezh Oblast, Russia. The population was 1,190 as of 2010. There are 11 streets.

## Geography

Nizhnemaryino is located 33 km north of Liski (the district's administrative centre) by road. Tresorukovo is the nearest rural locality.
