# Shinhopple, New York

Shinhopple, New York

**Shinhopple** is a hamlet in Delaware County, New York, United States. The community is located along the East Branch Delaware River and New York State Route 30, 9.6 miles (15.4 km) south-southeast of Walton. Shinhopple had a post office until July 31, 1993.
