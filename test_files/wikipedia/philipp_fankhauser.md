# Philipp Fankhauser

Philipp Fankhauser

**Philipp Manuel Fankhauser** (born 20 February 1964 in Thun, Switzerland) is a Swiss blues musician and songwriter. Several of his albums have charted in the top ten of the Swiss Hitparade, such as *Love Man Riding*, which peaked at No. 7, *Home*, which peaked at No. 2, *Try My Love*, which peaked at No. 3, and the collaborative live album *Unplugged - Live At Mühle Hunziken* with Margie Evans, which peaked at No. 4. His album *Heebie Jeebies - The Early Songs Of Johnny Copeland* jumped straight to number one at the end of 2022.
