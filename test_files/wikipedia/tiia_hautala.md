# Tiia Hautala

Tiia Hautala

**Tiia Erika Hautala** (born 3 April 1972 in Pori) is a Finnish former heptathlete.

In 1997, Hautala scored 7,233 points at a women's decathlon in Kangasala to set the Finnish record in the event.
